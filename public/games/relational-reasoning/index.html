📚 The Science

Relational integration — binding mental representations into complex structures — is the strongest predictor of fluid intelligence, explaining variance beyond working memory tasks (Chuderski, 2013). SMART training (Roche & Cassidy) uses relational frames (same/opposite, more/less, before/after) with increasing transitive complexity. Studies report IQ gains of 8–15+ points with dosages of 30–45 min, 2–3× weekly over 10–12 weeks (Cassidy et al., 2016; Colbert et al., 2018; McLoughlin et al., 2020). Nonsense syllables isolate pure relational skill. Negation and mixed relations add cognitive demand at advanced levels.
